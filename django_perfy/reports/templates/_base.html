Methodology & data integrity

Normal requests are sampled at ~10%; slow (≥ threshold) and error (≥ 400) requests are kept at 100%. All volume, rate and cost figures are inverse-probability weighted to reconstruct true totals. These are observed production measurements, not a synthetic load test; "peak" is the highest naturally occurring value in the window, not a tested ceiling.
